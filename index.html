Who are we?

The Puget Sound Socialist Rifle Association is a politically left firearm education and advocacy group. Our mission is to ensure that the working class of Washington have access to firearms, are skilled in their use, know safe handling and storage practices, and can act as responsible gun owners.

Not equipped? Not a problem!

Our organization has easy to follow equipment guides to get you started in acquiring the proper equipment to become a responsible gun owner, and our range coordinators can lend out ear and eye protection at range events

Education and Information for new and experienced shooters alike.

Our Subject Matter Experts provide skilled instruction and in depth, personalized gear recommendations for all weapons and equipment, and are available to teach our core pistol curriculum to new shooters, or to help hone the skills of experienced hands.

A strong community of socialists

In addition to practical firearm training the PSSRA connects members to a large network of fellow socialists with knowledge and expertise in a wide variety of subjects. Our community also provides an excellent place to network with other leftists, build community connections, and find common interests.

We Support your rights

The PSSRA firmly supports BIPOC and LGBTQ+ rights. We do not tolerate abusers; supporters of fascism or racism; supporters of exclusionary social ideologies such as ableism, transphobia, or homophobia; or members of conservative or reactionary movements (NRA membership is acceptable). At this time we cannot allow membership to law enforcement officers or those employed at domestic intelligence agencies.

We are not an activist organization

The SRA is purely and education and advocay organization. As such the PSSRA does not take part in any kind of direct action. Members may not attend protests under the SRA banner, nor we do show up to non-protest events armed. In addition we do not perform mutual aid or engage in community outreach programs that are not firearm education related. However, we do not have prohibitions against our members doing these activities privately or from joining orgs that are focused on mutual aid or community outreach.
